Methodology & caveats

  • Sentiment and topic were assigned by manual review of each comment's text (Indonesian-language, informal/slang), not a statistical classifier — treat splits as directional, not lab-grade precision.
  • @xlsmart's own customer-service reply comments (5) and 20 blank/emoji-stripped rows were excluded, leaving 173 of the original 193 scraped comments.
  • Apify's free-tier access capped comment retrieval at roughly 15 per post on most posts, so high-engagement posts are undersampled relative to their true comment count.
  • One 10x-repeated complaint about a 3-day signal outage inflates the Network Experience negative count — treat it as one viral incident, not ten independent complaints.
  • 2 of the 31 posts were published slightly before the intended 30-day window (Apify's date filter returned them regardless); included here since they still carry live customer commentary.
